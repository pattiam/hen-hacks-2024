Addition Lesson

Welcome to the Addition Lesson!

In this lesson, we will learn how to add numbers together.

What is Addition?

Addition is when we put numbers together to find out how many there are altogether.

Example:

If we have 2 apples and add 3 more apples, we have:

2 apples + 3 apples = 5 apples

How to Add:

  1. Start with the first number.
  2. Count on by saying the next number.
  3. Count all the numbers together to find the total.

Let's Practice:

Now, let's practice some addition problems!

Try adding these numbers together:

  • 1 + 2 = ___
  • 3 + 4 = ___
  • 5 + 6 = ___
  • 2 + 8 = ___
  • 4 + 5 = ___

Once you're done, you can check your answers with your teacher or a grown-up!
